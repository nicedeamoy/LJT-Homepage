I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate in the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/) at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Professor [Junxian He](https://jxhe.co/). My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with particular interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**

Prior to starting my Ph.D., I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024.

## News

- **2023**: Two papers accepted to NeurIPS 2023.
